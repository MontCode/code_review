    The Story of Jesus

    Birth and Early Childhood

  • 5 BC - Jesus is born to a virgin mother named Mary who is married to a man named Joseph in Bethleham.
    Luke 2:1-20 NIV
  • 4 BC - An angel appeared to Joseph in a dream and told him to flee to Egypt as Herod the King will seek to kill Jesus. Joseph and Mary take Jesus and escape to Egypt.
    Matthew 2:13-18 NIV
  • 4 BC - After Herod dies, Joseph and Mary are instructred to go to Israel. They eventually settle in Nazareth.
    Matthew 2:19-23 NIV
  • 8 AD - A young Jesus visits the temple in Jerusalem and amazes people in the temple courts by his understanding and his answers.
    Luke 2:41-52 NIV
